About Cupid’s Misfire

A quirky romantic comedy series, we follow the misadventures of Eloise, a hopeless romantic, and Alex, a cynical divorce lawyer, whose paths cross when a mischievous Cupid accidentally ties their fates together. As they navigate the chaos of love’s blunders, they discover that sometimes the wrong person can lead to the right feelings.

Creator:

Evelyn Archer, Liam Sterling, Aria Knight, Ethan Hunt, Isabella Chase, Mason Locke, Sophia Mercer, Oliver Pierce, Amelia Warren, Noah Ford.

Genres:

Romantic Comedy, Fantasy
