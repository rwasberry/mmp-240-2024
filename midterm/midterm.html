Cupid’s Misfire

A quirky romantic comedy series, we follow the misadventures of Eloise, a hopeless romantic, and Alex, a cynical divorce lawyer, whose paths cross
when a mischievous Cupid accidentally ties their fates together. As they navigate the chaos of love’s blunders,
they discover that sometimes the wrong person can lead to the right feelings.
